BD367281 - 21G x 3/4" needle x 12" tubing BD Safety-Lok™ blood collection and infusion set with luer adapter. Manually activated safety shield to fully cover needle (50/BX)

BD Safety-Lok™ Blood Collection Sets have been designed to shield healthcare workers from exposed needles. Following venipuncture, a yellow transparent shield is moved over the exposed needle and locked into protective position, creating a barrier between the healthcare professional and the used needle.
